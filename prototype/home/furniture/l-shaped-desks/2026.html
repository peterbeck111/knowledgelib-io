Important Caveats

  • Prices are approximate street prices as of April 2026. Amazon frequently offers coupons and lightning deals on furniture; Vari and UPLIFT run promotional discounts every 4-6 weeks.
  • Assembly is required for all models and typically takes 45-90 minutes for fixed desks, 90-180 minutes for electric standing desks. Two people are recommended for 90"+ desks and all standing desks.
  • Reversible L-shaped desks must be configured (left or right) during assembly. Changing orientation later requires full disassembly.
  • Weight capacity figures are for the entire desk surface. Concentrated loads on one corner may reduce stability.
  • Premium standing desks (UPLIFT, Vari) ship with 5-15 year frame warranties; Amazon-brand budget desks typically offer 1-2 years. Long-term cost-of-ownership often favors the premium tier despite the 5-10x price gap.
