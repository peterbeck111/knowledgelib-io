Important Caveats

  • Prices are approximate US retail as of April 2026 and fluctuate frequently on Amazon. SIHOO M18 currently shows $159.99 (down from $199.99 MSRP) — verify before purchase.
  • Foam cushions in sub-$150 chairs compress noticeably within 12-18 months of daily 8-hour use. Mesh seats last longer.
  • Warranties are typically 1-3 years at this price — significantly shorter than commercial-grade chairs ($300+). Hbada J3 includes a 3-year warranty (longest in this list at <$150).
  • Weight capacity claims on budget chairs may not be independently certified. SIHOO M18 with BIFMA certification is a notable exception. Ticova's 350 lbs claim is uncertified.
  • Colamy Atlas is referenced as a step-up option but exceeds the $200 ceiling ($280-300 typical). It is not a primary recommendation for this card's price tier.
  • True 4D armrests (height, width, depth, pivot independently) are not available under $200. All chairs listed here offer 2D, 3D, or flip-up armrests.
