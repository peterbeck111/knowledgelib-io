## Important Caveats

- Prices are approximate street prices as of April 2026. FlexiSpot and UPLIFT frequently run promotions during holidays and sales events; sale-driven 20-30% drops on these models are common.
- Frame-only models are often cheaper but require purchasing a separate desktop. Factor in $50-150 for a quality desktop if buying frame-only. The FlexiSpot E7 Plus and UPLIFT V3 are explicitly out of scope for this card (both exceed $500 even frame-only).
- Single-motor desks (Fezibo, ErGear) may wobble at full standing height, especially on carpet. Use leveling feet on uneven surfaces.
- The 1:2 sit-to-stand ratio (15-20 minutes standing per 30 minutes sitting) is recommended for health benefits. Standing all day is not better than sitting all day.
- Monitor arms are recommended over monitor stands for standing desks — they maintain eye-level positioning as the desk height changes.
- Manufacturer-stated weight capacities are peak ratings under controlled tests. Derate by ~20% for sustained daily use.
- Warranty coverage is often split: FlexiSpot's 15-year coverage applies to frame and motors but only 5 years on electronics/controllers. Always verify which components are covered.
