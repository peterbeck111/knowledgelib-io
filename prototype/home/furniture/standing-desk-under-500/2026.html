Important Caveats

  • Prices are approximate street prices as of April 2026. FlexiSpot and UPLIFT frequently run promotions; sale-driven 20-30% drops are common.
  • Frame-only models are cheaper but require purchasing a separate desktop ($50-150 extra). The FlexiSpot E7 Plus and UPLIFT V3 are explicitly out of scope (both exceed $500 even frame-only).
  • Single-motor desks may wobble at full standing height, especially on carpet.
  • The 1:2 sit-to-stand ratio (15-20 minutes standing per 30 minutes sitting) is recommended for health.
  • Monitor arms are recommended over monitor stands for standing desks — they maintain eye-level as height changes.
  • Manufacturer-stated weight capacities are peak ratings under controlled tests. Derate by ~20% for sustained daily use.
  • Warranty coverage is often split: FlexiSpot's 15-year coverage applies to frame and motors but only 5 years on electronics/controllers. Always verify which components are covered.
