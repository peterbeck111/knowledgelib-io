Important Caveats

  • Prices shown are approximate U.S. street prices as of April 2026. Power tool combo kits experience significant price fluctuations, particularly during holiday promotions (Black Friday, Father's Day, Spring Sale) when discounts of 30-59% are common at Home Depot, Lowe's, and Amazon.
  • Voltage numbers across brands are not directly comparable. DeWalt's "20V MAX" and Milwaukee's "18V" both produce the same nominal 18V output -- DeWalt markets the maximum voltage while Milwaukee uses the nominal voltage. Flex's 24V platform genuinely operates at a higher voltage than 18V/20V systems.
  • Battery capacity (Ah) directly impacts runtime and weight. Higher-capacity batteries (4.0Ah-5.0Ah) provide longer runtime but add 30-40% more weight to each tool. Consider whether the included batteries match your typical usage patterns.
  • Ridgid tools are exclusively available at Home Depot, Craftsman and Kobalt tools at Lowe's. This can limit purchase options and warranty service availability compared to brands sold through multiple retailers.
  • This comparison focuses on the U.S. market. International availability, pricing, and model numbers vary. Some kits may include different tool combinations in different regions.
