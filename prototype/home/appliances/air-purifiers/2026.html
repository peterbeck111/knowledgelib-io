Important Caveats

  • Prices are approximate U.S. street prices as of April 2026. Sales, promotions, and regional pricing vary significantly. Models like the Coway AP-1512HH regularly drop 20-30% during Amazon sales events.
  • CADR ratings are measured by AHAM using standardized tests with smoke, dust, and pollen particles. HouseFresh uses its own PM1 CADR methodology with incense testing in a controlled 728 sq ft room — their numbers may not directly compare to AHAM-certified CADR.
  • Room size ratings from manufacturers typically use 1 air change per hour (ACH). The EPA and AHAM recommend 4-5 ACH for effective purification. Divide the manufacturer's room size by 4-5 to estimate effective coverage.
  • Annual filter replacement costs range from $30 (Levoit Core 300S) to $365 (IQAir HealthPro Plus) and should be factored into total cost of ownership. Running a purifier 24/7 on medium adds $20-50/year in electricity.
  • HouseFresh dropped the Blueair Blue Pure 311i Max from its recommended list because the ionizer function cannot be disabled. Users concerned about trace ozone should verify ionizer disable capabilities before purchasing any model with ionizer technology.
