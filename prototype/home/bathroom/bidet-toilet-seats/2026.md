# Best Bidet Toilet Seats and Attachments (2026)

## Summary

The bidet market in 2026 spans three main categories: electric bidet seats with heated water and air dryers, non-electric bidet attachments that fit under existing seats, and non-electric bidet seats that replace the entire toilet seat. The biggest development this year is TOTO completing its transition to an all-tankless S-series lineup: the new S2 (~$499) replaces the entry-level C2 with tankless instant heating, the S5 (~$650) replaces the mid-range C5, and the flagship S7A (~$1,300) remains the industry's slimmest at 4.1 inches. The C5 (~$430) is being discontinued, though remaining stock is still available. [src1, src6, src8]

The Alpha JX2 (~$389) retains BidetKing's #1 overall ranking and CNN Underscored's Best Overall Bidet for five consecutive years (2021-2025). The Alpha UX Pearl (~$450) was named Best Overall by Yahoo Tested and Best Value by Popular Science, with its photocatalyst deodorizer and 4.5-inch profile. New for 2026, the BidetKing Crown (~$429) has emerged as a trending pick with the strongest spray pressure tested (1 L/min), a multicolor in-bowl LED, and a 3-year tiered warranty. The Tushy Cloud (~$359) marks Tushy's first electric bidet seat, featuring coil-heated endless warm water, UV nozzle sterilization, and heated seat/dryer — though it's elongated only. The Alpha iX Pure (~$349) offers the lowest entry price for a tankless bidet seat with a slim 4.5-inch profile. [src3, src4, src6]

For non-electric options, the Tushy Classic 3.0 (~$119) remains the most popular bidet attachment, praised for its comfortable stream and 8.5-minute installation, while the Luxe Bidet Neo 320 Plus (~$60) earned Reviewed's top attachment pick with dual nozzles and warm water via sink hookup. The Brondell EcoSeat S101 (~$90) splits the difference as a complete non-electric seat replacement with integrated dual nozzles and a sittable lid. [src2, src4, src7]

## Best for Each Use Case

### Best Overall: Alpha JX2 (~$389) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/alpha-jx2)
BidetKing's #1 overall pick and CNN Underscored's Best Overall Bidet for five consecutive years (2021-2025), plus Forbes Vetted's Best of 2026. The JX2 combines tankless water heating for endless warm water with bowl mist (added mid-2024), a stainless steel nozzle, LED nightlight, strong air dryer, and a sturdy sittable lid supporting 320 lbs. At ~$389 with a 3-year warranty, it delivers the core electric bidet experience without unnecessary complexity. [src3, src6]

### Best TOTO Mid-Range: TOTO Washlet S5 (~$650) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/toto-washlet-s5)
The S5 replaces the C5 with three major upgrades: tankless instantaneous water heating for endless warm water (eliminating the C5's 30-60 second tank limit), a 19% slimmer profile that hugs the bowl line, and a redesigned multifunction remote storing four user presets. It retains PREMIST, EWATER+ wand cleaning, heated seat, warm air dryer, and automatic deodorizer. At ~$650, it sits between the S2 and S7A in TOTO's new all-tankless lineup. [src6, src8]

### Best TOTO Entry: TOTO Washlet S2 (~$499) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/toto-washlet-s2)
New for 2026, the S2 replaces the tank-based C2 as TOTO's entry-level S-series seat. CNN Underscored's tester called it the most comfortable stream and dryer tested, with endless warm water from its tankless heater. It includes PREMIST, EWATER+ wand cleaning, heated seat, warm air dryer, and deodorizer — the same core TOTO tech as the S5 and S7A. The trade-off: no user presets and a side-panel control instead of a remote. [src3, src8]

### Best Luxury: TOTO Washlet S7A (~$1,300) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/toto-washlet-s7a)
TOTO's flagship stands just 4.1 inches tall at the rear — the slimmest bidet seat on the market. The auto-open and auto-close lid senses your approach and departure, while a built-in nightlight illuminates the bathroom for nighttime use. EWATER+ mists both the wand and the toilet bowl, reducing cleaning frequency. The remote stores up to four personalized wash settings. Available in contemporary and classic lid styles in Cotton White or Sedona Beige. [src3, src6]

### Best Value Premium: Alpha UX Pearl (~$450) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/alpha-ux-pearl)
Winner of Best Overall from Yahoo Tested and Best Value from Popular Science, the Alpha UX Pearl uses a tankless instant water heating system for truly endless warm water. The arced stainless steel nozzle provides one of the strongest and most comfortable washes tested. At just 4.5 inches tall with an LED nightlight featuring an ambient sensor, backlit side control knob, and photocatalyst deodorizer, the UX Pearl delivers luxury-tier features at a mid-range price. The sittable lid supports up to 320 pounds, and a 3-year warranty backs the unit. [src4, src5]

### Best Spray Pressure: BidetKing Crown (~$429) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/bidetking-crown)
Trending at #5 on BidetKing's 2026 rankings, the Crown delivers up to 1 L/min of aerated spray — the highest spray pressure of any bidet seat tested. At just 4 inches tall, its ultra-slim profile rivals the Brondell T44. The tankless heater provides endless warm water, while the industry's first multicolor in-bowl LED and "Party Mode" set it apart aesthetically. Includes heated seat, warm air dryer, deodorizer, 2 user presets, and a backlit remote. The 3-year tiered warranty covers 100% of parts/labor in year one, 75% in year two, and 50% in year three. Supports 320 lbs. [src6]

### Best Mainstream Electric: Tushy Cloud (~$359) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/tushy-cloud)
Tushy's first electric bidet seat brings coil-heated endless warm water, a heated seat (3 levels), warm air dryer (3 levels), and UV nozzle sterilization to the sub-$400 segment. CNN Underscored found it one of the most comfortable bidets tested, behind only TOTO's lineup. Five pressure levels cover gentle to powerful. The major limitation: elongated version only — no round option. A strong choice for Tushy Classic upgraders wanting heated water without a $500+ jump. [src3]

### Best for Sensitive Users: Brondell Swash 1400 (~$550) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/brondell-swash-1400)
The Brondell Swash 1400 offers the gentlest wash cycle of any bidet seat tested, making it ideal for users with sensitivity concerns, postpartum recovery, or medical conditions. Seven nozzle positions with adjustable spray width, along with endless warm water from a hybrid heating system, let users dial in exactly the right wash. The carbon deodorizer, LED nightlight, and programmable user settings via wireless remote add convenience. The sittable lid supports up to 270 pounds. A 3-year limited warranty is included. [src3, src6]

### Best Ultra-Slim: Brondell Swash Thinline T44 (~$560) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/brondell-swash-t44)
At 3.9 inches (99mm) tall, the T44 is the slimmest bidet seat after the TOTO S7A (4.1 inches). Its ceramic instant water heater provides endless warm water, while 5 pressure settings, 4 temperature levels, and 5 nozzle positions offer full customization. The 7-color LED nightlight (not just single-color), stainless steel self-cleaning nozzle, and 2-user preset remote round out the feature set. A 3-year tiered residential warranty is included. [src6]

### Best for Heavy/Bariatric Users: Bio Bidet BB-2000 (~$450) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/bio-bidet-bb-2000)
With a 420-lb weight capacity — the highest in this roundup — the Bio Bidet BB-2000 is the top choice for bariatric users. Its vortex wash produces spray 30% stronger than competitors, and the 3-in-1 stainless steel nozzle handles rear, front, and enema functions. Hybrid heating, heated seat, and warm air dryer are included. A 3-year full warranty (optional 6-year extended) provides long-term coverage. The Eco Nova (~$699, 400 lbs) is the premium alternative with UV sterilization. [src6]

### Best Budget Tankless: Alpha iX Pure (~$349) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/alpha-ix-pure)
The iX Pure is the lowest-priced tankless bidet seat in this roundup, offering endless warm water, an arced stainless steel nozzle, heated seat, warm air dryer, and a blue LED nightlight with ambient sensor — all in a 4.5-inch slim profile. The simplified remote with Easy Wash and Auto Wash/Dry shortcuts makes it beginner-friendly. Supports 320 lbs with a sittable lid. The 1-year warranty is shorter than Alpha's JX2 and UX Pearl (both 3 years), which is the main trade-off for the lower price. [src6]

### Best Budget Electric Seat: Brondell Swash LE99 (~$250) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/brondell-swash-le99)
Wirecutter's budget pick, the Brondell Swash LE99 packs a surprising feature set at an entry-level price. The hybrid warm water system provides heated washes, while strong and gentle wash modes accommodate different preferences. Five nozzle positions, five water temperature settings, and five pressure levels offer customization, controlled via a Lite-Touch wireless remote. The dedicated gentle mode makes it family-friendly and safe for children. [src1, src5]

### Best Non-Electric Attachment: Tushy Classic 3.0 (~$119) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/tushy-classic-3)
The Tushy Classic 3.0 is the most popular bidet attachment in America, praised for its comfortable stream, adjustable spray angle, and quick 8.5-minute installation. The patented Self-Cleaning SmartSpray nozzle rinses before and after each use, while the Schmutz Shield prevents grime buildup. As a cold-water-only attachment, it requires no electricity or plumbing modifications beyond connecting to the toilet's existing water supply. [src2, src4]
