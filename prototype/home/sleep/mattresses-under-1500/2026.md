## Important Caveats

- Prices listed reflect common sale prices as of April 2026; MSRP may be 25-50% higher. Mattress brands run frequent promotions (Sleep Week, Memorial Day, Black Friday), so check current pricing before purchasing.
- Firmness is subjective and varies by body weight, sleep position, and personal preference. A mattress rated "medium" by one reviewer may feel different to individual sleepers. Test in-store if possible.
- Most review sites receive affiliate commissions from mattress brands, which may influence placement. This unit cross-references 8 independent testing sources to reduce bias.
- Trial periods require sleeping on the mattress for a minimum break-in period (typically 30 nights) before returns are accepted. Return shipping fees ($99-$199) may apply with some brands; Saatva charges a $99 return processing fee.
- Mattress construction and pricing can change without notice. Verify current specifications and pricing directly with the manufacturer before purchasing.
- Buying mattresses on Amazon may offer different return/trial terms than buying direct from the brand. Verify trial period and warranty coverage before purchasing through third-party retailers.
