Important Caveats

  • Prices are approximate US street/sale prices as of April 2026. MSRPs are often 40-60% higher, and pricing fluctuates with promotions and regional availability. International pricing varies significantly.
  • Suction power in Pascals (Pa) is a manufacturer-reported metric that lacks standardized testing methodology across brands. Vacuum Wars' independent airflow measurements (in CFM/kPa) provide more reliable cross-brand comparisons. The MOVA P50 Pro Ultra measured only 0.71 kPa despite claiming 19,000Pa.
  • iRobot completed its sale to Picea Robotics on January 23, 2026. While the company claims continued US operations and data protection, the long-term product roadmap and software support remain uncertain under new ownership.
  • Robot vacuums with mopping capabilities are supplemental cleaning tools. Even the best roller-mop robots struggle with dried or ground-in stains compared to manual mopping or dedicated mop robots.
  • The MOVA P10 Pro Ultra Gen 2 ($499-$599) and Ecovacs Deebot T80 Omni ($499 on sale) sit at the boundary of this price tier and may exceed $500 outside of promotional periods.
