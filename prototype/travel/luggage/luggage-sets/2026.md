# Best Luggage Sets (2026)

## Summary

The luggage set market in 2026 is defined by the rise of premium polycarbonate hardside sets with smart features, while budget-friendly ABS options have also dramatically improved in quality. July's Trunk Set remains the top overall premium pick, combining zipperless German polycarbonate construction with ultra-quiet SilentMove spinner wheels and built-in power banks, earning acclaim from multiple reviewers for durability and design [src1, src3]. The new July Carry On with CaseSafe now includes Find My and Find Hub luggage tracking, signaling that smart-tracking is becoming a standard feature in premium sets [src4]. CNN Underscored's April 2026 roundup crowned the **Calpak Evry Starter Bundle (~$299, up from $294)** as best overall, citing its complete five-packing-cube accessory kit and sub-$300 price as category-defining value [src5].

The direct-to-consumer revolution has matured, with brands like Away, Monos, and July offering 2-piece and 3-piece matching sets at significant discounts compared to buying individual pieces. Away's Bigger Carry-On + Large set saves $50-$100 versus individual purchases, while Monos bundles save up to $150 [src1, src4]. For 2026, Away's new **Jetsetter Bundle** (~$915) adds a true 3-piece hardside option (carry-on + medium + large), while the Beis Starter Set (~$320) features a built-in weight indicator and 100% virgin polycarbonate construction at mid-range pricing [src4, src5, src6]. CNN's best-softside pick, the **Travelpro Maxlite 5 3-Piece Set (~$574)**, offers weather-resistant polyester, 2-inch expansion, and a limited lifetime warranty — the softside value leader for 2026 [src5].

A key trend for Q2 2026 is the integration of tech features as standard rather than premium add-ons: GPS/Bluetooth tracking, built-in scales, and Bluetooth locks are appearing across price points [src2, src6]. Sustainability is also front and center, with brands like Travelpro using 100% post-consumer recycled plastic bottle linings and Monos offering vegan leather details throughout their collections [src1, src2]. Meanwhile, traditional powerhouses Samsonite and Travelpro have responded with updated models and expanded online-exclusive sets to compete with DTC brands on both price and features [src1, src7, src8].

## Best for Each Use Case

### Best Overall Value: Calpak Evry Starter Bundle (~$299) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/calpak-evry-starter-bundle)
CNN Underscored's best-overall pick for 2026, the Evry Starter Bundle includes a large checked bag, a carry-on, a five-piece packing cube set, and a luggage tag — the most complete package in this comparison. The polycarbonate shells feature expandable zippers (up to 2 inches), TSA-approved locks, and cushioned handles. Available in eye-catching colors including Raspberry, Lemon Yellow, and Persimmon Orange. Regularly discounted from $545 to ~$299 on the Calpak website. [src4, src5]

### Best Premium Overall: July Trunk Set (~$1,075) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/july-trunk-set)
July's Trunk Set earns the top premium spot with its zipperless twin-latch design that offers superior security and a wider 80/20 opening compared to traditional zipper suitcases. The German polycarbonate shell is aerospace-grade, making it extremely impact-resistant while remaining lightweight at 8.6 lbs for the carry-on. The 2026 update adds CaseSafe tracking (compatible with Apple Find My and Google Find Hub networks), SilentMove 360-degree spinner wheels, an ejectable battery, a 20-height telescoping handle, water-and-stain-proof lining, and removable laundry bags. Available in 12 colors. [src1, src3, src4]

### Best Budget: Coolife 3-Piece Hardshell Set (~$130) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/coolife-3-piece-hardshell-set)
At around $130 for all three sizes (20", 24", and 28"), the Coolife set is virtually unbeatable on price-per-piece value. The ABS hardshell construction is surprisingly durable for the price, featuring TSA-approved combination locks, YKK zippers rated for 3,000+ cycles, and four 360-degree multi-directional spinner wheels on each piece. While it lacks premium features like expandable zippers or advanced compression systems, it covers the essentials for occasional travelers. [src1, src2]

### Best Hardside 2-Piece: Away Bigger Carry-On + Large Set (~$625) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/away-2-piece-set)
Away practically defined the direct-to-consumer hardside luggage category, and their updated 2-piece set (Bigger Carry-On + Large) remains a benchmark. The impact-resistant polycarbonate shell, whisper-quiet 360-degree wheels, and built-in TSA lock are complemented by a removable USB battery in the carry-on for mid-transit charging. Away's 2026 lineup adds mix-and-match color options and personalization. The interior compression system maximizes packing space, and the set price saves approximately $50 versus buying pieces individually. [src1, src2, src4]

### Best Hardside 3-Piece: Away Jetsetter Bundle (~$915) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/away-jetsetter-bundle)
New for 2026 from Away, the Jetsetter Bundle combines a carry-on, medium, and large checked suitcase — Away's first true 3-piece bundle. CNN Underscored names it best hard-shell pick for 2026, highlighting the signature interior compression system, smooth-rolling wheels, and lifetime warranty that covers cracks, dents, wheel damage, and handle issues. 10 personalization options and a regular bundle price of $1,015 (currently ~$915 on sale) save roughly $100 vs buying pieces individually. [src5]

### Best Softside 2-Piece: Travelpro Platinum Elite 2-Piece Set (~$680) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/travelpro-platinum-elite-2-piece-set)
Favored by flight crews and frequent business travelers, the Platinum Elite set uses high-density nylon with DuraGuard coating for water, stain, and abrasion resistance. The patented MagnaTrac self-aligning wheels are among the smoothest in the industry, and the fold-out suiter compartment keeps garments wrinkle-free. Additional features include a built-in USB port, external zip compartments, and a Contour Grip handle. [src1, src2, src3]

### Best Softside 3-Piece: Travelpro Maxlite 5 3-Piece Set (~$574) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/travelpro-maxlite-5-3-piece-set)
CNN Underscored's best soft-side pick for 2026. The Maxlite 5 set delivers three pieces (21" carry-on, 25" medium, 29" large) in weather-resistant polyester with exterior pockets, 2-inch expansion, and lightweight construction (6.1 lbs for the carry-on). Backed by Travelpro's limited lifetime warranty. Significantly cheaper than Platinum Elite and ideal for travelers who want softside flexibility at a mid-range price. [src5]

### Most Durable: Briggs & Riley Torq 2-Piece Set (~$1,100) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/briggs-riley-torq-2-piece-set)
Backed by a no-questions-asked lifetime warranty that even covers airline damage, the Briggs & Riley Torq set is built for decades of use. The three-layer Makrolon polycarbonate shell resists impact and flexing, while self-repairing double zippers and an aircraft-grade aluminum handle system ensure long-term reliability. The medium spinner includes a mesh suiter with anti-wrinkle cinchers and a leather nameplate. [src1, src3]

### Best Design Features: Beis The Starter Set (~$320) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/beis-starter-set)
Beis offers 100% virgin polycarbonate construction at mid-range pricing — a material typically found on sets costing $500+. The standout feature is a built-in weight indicator on the side handle that signals when your bag exceeds 50 lbs, eliminating overweight fees. Interior compartments function as built-in packing cubes, 360-degree spinner wheels roll smoothly, and the weekender-variant Starter Set features an internal lock and expandable weekender compartment. [src4, src6]

### Best Lightweight Hardside: Travelpro Maxlite Air 3-Piece Set (~$512) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/travelpro-maxlite-air-3-piece-set)
When every pound matters, the Maxlite Air delivers all three sizes (21", 25", and 28") at remarkably low weights: just 6.3 lbs for the carry-on. Despite the featherweight construction, the polycarbonate shell is engineered to flex on impact without cracking. The eco-friendly 100% recycled polyester lining, TSA locks, and PowerScope Lite handle that locks in two positions round out an impressive feature set at a competitive price. [src1, src2, src7]

### Best Mid-Tier Value: Samsonite Winfield 2 3-Piece Set (~$350) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/samsonite-winfield-2-3-piece-set)
The Winfield 2 set delivers genuine Samsonite quality at a surprisingly accessible price point. The impact-absorbing polycarbonate shell is scratch-resistant with a brushed finish, and each piece features 2-inch expansion capability, Y-strap compression, and four multi-directional spinner wheels. With 20", 24", and 28" sizes, it covers everything from weekend getaways to extended international trips. [src1, src2, src4]
