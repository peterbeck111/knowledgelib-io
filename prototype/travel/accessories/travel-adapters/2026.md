## Key Market Trends (2026)

- **Dual-module GaN is the new high-power standard**: TESSAN Voyager 205 introduced independent 160W + 45W power rails so multiple devices charge at full speed simultaneously rather than splitting a shared budget. Expect Anker, Ugreen, and Belkin imitators by H2 2026. [src7, src8]
- **PD 3.1 (140W+) trickles down to travel adapters**: 2024 PD 3.1 was reserved for desk chargers; in 2026 the TESSAN 140W and Voyager 205 brought it to travel form factors. Required for MacBook Pro 16" full-speed charging. [src7, src8]
- **Anker Nano displaces EPICKA at Wirecutter**: After 30+ hours of testing 14 models, Wirecutter is moving its top pick from EPICKA TA-105 Original to the Anker Nano in their forthcoming March 2026 guide update. Anker's Nano has also fallen to $20 on Amazon. [src9]
- **GaN technology goes mainstream**: Gallium Nitride adapters now deliver 65-205W from pocket-sized form factors, eliminating separate laptop chargers for USB-C laptop travelers. [src1, src4]
- **USB-C dominance accelerates**: Most new adapters now ship with 2-6 USB-C ports versus 1-2 in 2024. The Voyager 205 ships with 6:1 USB-C to USB-A ratio. USB-A is becoming a legacy compatibility port. [src2, src5, src7]
- **Price compression**: A 70W GaN universal adapter costs ~$35 in 2026, down from ~$60 in 2024. Budget 20W adapters have dropped below $20. [src3, src4, src9]
- **Safety certification emphasis**: RoHS, CE, FCC certifications are now table stakes. Brands without visible certification marks are being flagged by review sites. [src4, src6]

## Important Caveats

- Travel adapters do NOT convert voltage. US/Canada 110-120V outlets differ from 220-240V in Europe/Asia. Ensure your devices support dual voltage (input: 100-240V) before plugging in.
- USB wattage is shared across ports on single-rail adapters. A 70W single-rail adapter splits power when charging multiple devices simultaneously; the Voyager 205's dual independent rails are the exception.
- No universal adapter covers every country. India (Type D) and South Africa (Type M) are commonly excluded.
- GaN adapters run warmer than traditional adapters — this is normal and within safety specifications.
- TESSAN Voyager 205 is currently sold direct via tessan.com (~$89-119) and via select retailers; Amazon availability is limited as of April 2026.
- Prices are Amazon US pricing as of April 2026 and may vary by retailer and region.
