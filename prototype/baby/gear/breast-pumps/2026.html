Important Caveats

  • Prices shown are approximate U.S. retail (April 2026) and may vary by retailer, bundle, and insurance coverage. Many double electric pumps are available at no cost through insurance.
  • The Eufy S2 Pro shipped to early-bird pre-orders in mid-April 2026; broad retail availability and final pricing may shift over the following weeks. Amazon ASIN was not yet listed at the time of this update.
  • Flange sizing is critical and highly personal. Most pumps ship with 24mm flanges, but 40-70% of women need a different size. Incorrect sizing causes pain, reduced output, and potential tissue damage. Measure or consult a lactation consultant before purchasing.
  • Individual results vary significantly with breast pumps. Suction strength, flange fit, and output are highly personal; what works well for one person may not work for another.
  • Manufacturer claims like "20% faster pumping" (VibraPump) and "35% supply boost" (HeatFlow 2.0) are vendor-stated; independent third-party lab confirmation typically takes 6-12 months post-launch.
  • This comparison focuses on the U.S. market. Model availability, pricing, and insurance coverage differ by region.
  • Wearable pump capacity (typically 4-5 oz per cup) is smaller than traditional pump bottles, requiring more frequent emptying during longer sessions.
  • Battery life figures are manufacturer-stated and real-world performance may vary based on suction level, session duration, and ambient temperature.
  • Always consult a lactation consultant for personalized pump recommendations, especially if experiencing supply issues or discomfort.
