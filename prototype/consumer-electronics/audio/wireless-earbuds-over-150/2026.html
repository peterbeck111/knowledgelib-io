Important Caveats

  • Prices are approximate US street prices as of April 2026. Regional pricing and sales vary 10-25%.
  • Hi-res codec support depends on your source device. LDAC requires Android or a compatible DAP. AirPods Pro 3 and Beats Powerbeats Pro 2 are AAC-only — fine for Apple devices, no hi-res wireless option.
  • ANC and bass response vary significantly with ear-tip seal. Try multiple tip sizes.
  • SoundGuys measured ANC percentages (90% AirPods Pro 3, 88% XM6, etc.) are averages across all frequencies; rankings shift by frequency band. Do not over-index on a single number.
  • The Sony WF-1000XM5 at ~$199 is now the best value in premium earbuds; the XM6 upgrade is meaningful but not transformative for casual listeners.
  • Bowers & Wilkins Pi8 battery (5h 10min measured by SoundGuys) is significantly below B&W's 6.5h claim — plan for frequent case top-ups.
  • Bose QC Ultra Earbuds 2 battery (6h ANC) remains the weakest in the premium tier — not ideal for long-haul travel without case access.
