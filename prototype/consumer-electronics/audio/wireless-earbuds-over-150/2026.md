# Best Wireless Earbuds Over $150 (2026)

## Summary

The premium wireless earbuds market above $150 continues to shift in 2026. The Sony WF-1000XM6 (~$299, down from $329 at launch) remains the top-ranked earbud on RTINGS and SoundGuys, delivering 88% average noise reduction, a 4.8/5 sound quality score, and 9h 41min measured battery with ANC on. The Technics EAH-AZ100 (~$248) remains the consensus pick for best multipoint + battery with category-leading 10h 40min ANC battery and triple-point Bluetooth. Samsung Galaxy Buds 4 Pro (~$250) launched in March 2026, replacing the Buds 3 Pro as Samsung's flagship with ANC 2.0 and Hi-Res Audio. [src1, src2, src6, src7, src8]

For noise cancellation, the Apple AirPods Pro 3 (~$249) now lead with SoundGuys' highest-ever measured ANC at 90% average noise reduction — ahead of the Sony XM6 (88%) and Bose QC Ultra 2 (~$279). The Bose QC Ultra Earbuds 2nd Gen still deliver the most subjectively immersive ANC silencing, though battery remains a weak point at 6h. For audiophile fidelity, the Noble Fokus Amadeus (~$349, down from $369) offers studio-grade detail and 8h with ANC on. [src1, src2, src3, src5, src9]

## Best for Each Use Case

### Best Overall: Sony WF-1000XM6 (~$299) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/sony-wf-1000xm6)
Now ~$299 (down from $329 at launch), the XM6 are RTINGS' top-ranked wireless earbuds and SoundGuys' best overall pick. SoundGuys measured 88% average noise reduction and a 4.8/5 MDAQS sound quality score. New 8.4mm driver, dual noise sensors sampling at 50,000 Hz, and a redesigned (11% smaller) body. Tested 9h 41min battery with ANC on. Bluetooth 5.3 with LDAC, LC3, and Auracast. [src1, src2, src6, src7, src8]

### Best ANC: Bose QuietComfort Ultra Earbuds 2nd Gen (~$279) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/bose-qc-ultra-earbuds-2)
Now ~$279 (down from $299 at launch). Bose's second-generation QC Ultra still deliver the most immersive noise silencing experience in any earbuds, with improved ActiveSense adaptive ANC and AI-based call noise suppression. Bluetooth 5.4 with LE Audio. Immersive Audio with head tracking. The 6h ANC battery is the trade-off — bring the case if you commute long distances. [src2, src3, src5]

### Best for Apple Users: Apple AirPods Pro 3 (~$249) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/apple-airpods-pro-3)
Apple's H3 chip delivers the best ANC SoundGuys has ever measured — 90% average noise reduction, ahead of Sony's XM6. Built-in heart-rate sensor for workouts and real-time live translation across iOS-supported languages. SoundGuys tested 8h 42min battery with ANC on. IP57 water/dust resistance. Seamless Apple ecosystem switching. [src2, src3, src5]

### Best Multipoint + Battery: Technics EAH-AZ100 (~$248) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/technics-eah-az100)
Now ~$248 (down from $299 at launch). Magnetic Fluid Driver technology delivers exceptional clarity. Triple-point Bluetooth multipoint (3 devices simultaneously) is unmatched at this price. 10h 40min ANC battery still leads the segment. LDAC and LC3 for hi-res wireless. Dolby Atmos with head tracking. [src1, src2]

### Best Value Flagship: Sony WF-1000XM5 (~$199 discounted) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/sony-wf-1000xm5)
With the XM6 settled at ~$299, the XM5 has dropped further to ~$199 at major US retailers — extraordinary value for a recent flagship. ANC, sound, and codec support remain excellent. Best price-to-performance flagship in the segment for those who don't need the XM6's latest driver and ANC tuning. [src2, src5, src6]

### Best for Samsung: Samsung Galaxy Buds 4 Pro (~$250) — [Check price](https://knowledgelib.io/go/samsung-galaxy-buds-4-pro)
Launched March 2026, replacing the Galaxy Buds 3 Pro. ANC 2.0 with 84% measured noise reduction (SoundGuys). 2-way speaker system with Hi-Res Audio. Real-time AI Live Translate and Interpreter mode require a Galaxy phone with One UI 7+. IP57 rating is the highest in the comparison. 4.8/5 MDAQS sound quality score per SoundGuys. Secure ear-hook design stays locked during intense workouts. Optical heart-rate sensor. SoundGuys measured 83% ANC and 10h battery. 45h total with case — among the longest in this comparison. IPX4 water resistance. H2 chip with ANC and transparency mode. [src2, src3, src5]

12mm carbon cone drivers with 32-bit DSP. aptX Lossless for CD-quality wireless. Charging case accepts USB-C or 3.5mm input and rebroadcasts to the buds — ideal for airplane IFE systems. SoundGuys measured 5h 10min battery (below B&W's 6.5h claim), so carry the case. IP54 rated. [src2, src3, src4]

## Important Caveats

- Prices are approximate US street prices as of April 2026. Regional pricing and sales vary 10-25%.
- Hi-res codec support depends on your source device. LDAC requires Android or a compatible DAP. AirPods Pro 3 and Beats Powerbeats Pro 2 are AAC-only — fine for Apple devices, no hi-res wireless option.
- ANC and bass response vary significantly with ear-tip seal. Try multiple tip sizes.
- SoundGuys measured ANC percentages (90% AirPods Pro 3, 88% XM6, etc.) are averages across all frequencies; rankings shift by frequency band. Do not over-index on a single number.
- The Sony WF-1000XM5 at ~$199 is now the best value in premium earbuds; the XM6 upgrade is meaningful but not transformative for casual listeners.
- Bowers & Wilkins Pi8 battery (5h 10min measured by SoundGuys) is significantly below B&W's 6.5h claim — plan for frequent case top-ups.
- Bose QC Ultra Earbuds 2 battery (6h ANC) remains the weakest in the premium tier — not ideal for long-haul travel without case access.
