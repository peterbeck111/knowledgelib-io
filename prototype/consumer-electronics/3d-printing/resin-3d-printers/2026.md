## Important Caveats

- Prices shown are approximate U.S. retail prices as of late April 2026. Sales, bundles, and regional pricing vary. Elegoo and Anycubic frequently run 20-40% promotions.
- Resin 3D printing requires post-processing: printed parts must be washed in isopropyl alcohol (or water-washable resin solvent) and UV-cured. A wash-and-cure station ($80-200) is a near-essential accessory not included in printer prices.
- Uncured resin is toxic and a skin sensitizer. Nitrile gloves, adequate ventilation, and safe disposal are mandatory. Many newer printers include air purifiers, but dedicated ventilation is still recommended.
- Build volume does not equal usable volume in all cases. Tilt release mechanisms, support structures, and resin drainage reduce effective print area by 5-15%.
- Print speed claims are maximum figures using high-speed resin and optimal settings. Real-world speeds with standard resin are typically 30-50% lower.
