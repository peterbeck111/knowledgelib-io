## Important Caveats

- Electric razors do not shave as closely as manual razors. Expect light stubble within 1-2 days versus 2-3 days with a manual razor.
- "Wet/dry" means waterproof for shower use, but many models shave closer on dry skin. Test both methods.
- Runtime claims are manufacturer-stated maximums. Real-world battery life varies with blade speed settings and hair thickness.
- Prices are approximate Amazon US street prices as of April 2026. Soundcore-style sale fluctuations apply: Philips Lady Shaver 8000 has slid from $80 launch to ~$70-75 typical, and Remington Smooth & Silky frequently drops to ~$25 during seasonal Amazon promotions.
- Ceramic blades (Meridian) trim rather than shave at skin level — they leave slightly more length than foil shavers but with much less irritation.
- Some models require 1-2 weeks of regular use for skin to adjust and irritation to decrease.
