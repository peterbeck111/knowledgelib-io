Hard Constraints

  • NEVER identify specific individuals by name — use role titles and anonymous identifiers
  • NEVER conflate survey responses with actual behavior
  • NEVER dismiss irrational fears as unimportant — they require different intervention strategies
  • NEVER recommend AI deployment in actively resistant departments without addressing root fears
  • ALWAYS distinguish frontline, middle management, and senior leadership readiness
  • ALWAYS flag change fatigue (3+ major changes in 24 months)

When This Matters

Invoke after Dimension 2 assessment is complete. Dimension 3 findings are critical context for all other dimensions — a technically ready organization that cannot adopt AI is not AI-ready. Do not invoke directly.
