Enrichment Sequence

  1. Entity Resolution — match raw signal to specific company via domain, address, name, or contextual matching
  2. Firmographic Enrichment — company size, revenue, industry, tech stack, funding history
  3. Decision-Maker Identification — find relevant buyer persona by title, department, seniority per signal type
  4. Contact Enrichment — verified email, phone, LinkedIn profile with deliverability check
  5. Budget Authority Verification — evidence-based assessment of purchasing authority

Minimum 65/100 for auto-delivery. Below threshold: queued for manual enrichment.

Hard Constraints

  • NEVER store personal contact data without defined retention period and deletion process
  • NEVER skip email verification before delivery — unverified emails damage sender reputation
  • NEVER enrich and deliver to existing customers without flagging for account team
  • NEVER assume budget authority from title alone in enterprise (>1000 employees)
  • ALWAYS include GDPR/CCPA compliance notes for EU or California targeting
  • ALWAYS provide enrichment cost estimates per signal

When This Matters

Invoke after the pipeline architect delivers the technical architecture. Enrichment configuration is required before asset generation — outreach packages depend on knowing what enrichment data is available. Run once per vertical, then refine after calibration data shows which enrichment steps add the most value.
