Revenue Operations Assessment

How mature is RevOps — quote-to-cash, billing accuracy, revenue recognition (ASC 606/IFRS 15)?

Type: Assessment Confidence: 0.84 Sources: 6 Verified: 2026-03-10

Purpose

This assessment evaluates the maturity of revenue operations across five dimensions: quote-to-cash process, billing accuracy, revenue recognition compliance (ASC 606/IFRS 15), systems integration, and RevOps analytics. The output identifies where revenue leakage occurs and where compliance risks exist. [src1]

Constraints

  • Requires billing system data, contract data, and revenue recognition schedules
  • Most relevant for B2B companies with subscription, usage-based, or multi-element arrangements [src2]
  • ASC 606/IFRS 15 complexity varies with contract structure
  • Assessment is diagnostic only — identifies gaps, does not prescribe system solutions
  • Re-run semi-annually or when pricing model or system changes occur

Common Pitfalls in Assessment

  • Q2C speed vs. accuracy trade-off: Optimizing for speed sometimes sacrifices billing accuracy. [src2]
  • Rev rec policy vs. practice divergence: Audit a sample of recent complex deals to verify policy coverage. [src3]
  • Systems integration underestimation: Every pricing model change requires integration updates. [src4]
