Accounts Payable & Receivable Diagnostic

How healthy are AP/AR — aging benchmarks, payment terms optimization, collection effectiveness?

Type: Assessment Confidence: 0.85 Sources: 6 Verified: 2026-03-10

Purpose

This assessment evaluates the health and maturity of AP/AR operations across five dimensions: receivables management, payables optimization, collections effectiveness, automation maturity, and controls compliance. The output identifies where cash is leaking and which improvements deliver the fastest working capital gains. [src3]

Constraints

  • Requires AR/AP aging reports, DSO/DPO history, and bad debt write-off data
  • Benchmarks vary dramatically by industry — always use industry-specific comparisons [src1]
  • This assessment is primarily B2B-focused; B2C has fundamentally different AR dynamics
  • Assessment is diagnostic only — identifies inefficiencies, does not prescribe vendor solutions
  • Re-run quarterly; AP/AR metrics shift with customer mix and seasonal patterns

Common Pitfalls in Assessment

  • DSO in isolation: Compare DSO to payment terms. 55-day DSO is excellent for Net 60 but terrible for Net 30. [src3]
  • DPO maximization trap: Stretching DPO too far damages supplier relationships. [src4]
  • Automation without process fix: Automating bad processes just gets bad results faster. [src6]
