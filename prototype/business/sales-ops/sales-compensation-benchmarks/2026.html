Constraints

Common Misinterpretations

  • OTE without geography adjustment: $150K in SF is below-market; in Omaha it is above-market. Always normalize. [src2]
  • OTE as expected earnings: With 31% attainment, median actual is 70-85% of OTE. Use expected earnings for budgeting.
  • Single-segment splits universally: 50:50 works for mid-market AEs but is wrong for SDRs (too risky) and enterprise AEs (too conservative).
  • Ignoring total comp: Equity and benefits can be 20-40% of total comp at startups. Compare total comp, not just OTE.
