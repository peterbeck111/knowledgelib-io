Pipeline Health Diagnostic

How healthy is a sales pipeline — stage distribution, aging, conversion rates, source mix with red flags?

Type: Assessment Confidence: 0.85 Sources: 6 Verified: 2026-03-09

Purpose

Evaluates B2B sales pipeline health across five dimensions: coverage adequacy, stage distribution, deal aging, conversion rates, and source diversity. Identifies red flags and routes to specific improvement actions. Focus is on the pipeline itself — deals, quality, progression, and conversion likelihood. [src1]

Constraints

  • Requires 6+ months pipeline data (12 preferred)
  • CRM data quality is the prerequisite — assess CRM first if unreliable
  • Pipeline health is relative to selling motion and deal sizes
  • Point-in-time diagnostic — run quarterly for trend analysis
  • Identifies problems, not root causes — use complementary cards for diagnosis

Common Pitfalls

  • Coverage inflation: Companies report 4x coverage but real coverage after removing stale deals is 2.5x. Always clean before measuring. [src2]
  • Ignoring source quality: 4x from inbound (30% win rate) is worth more than 4x from cold outbound (15%). Weight by source conversion.
  • Snapshot instead of trend: A healthy pipeline today can mask declining generation. Assess trajectory, not just current state.
  • Confusing activity with quality: Pipeline up 20% but win rates down 30% = quality degradation, not health improvement.
