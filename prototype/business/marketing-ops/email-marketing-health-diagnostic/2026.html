Email Marketing Health Diagnostic

How healthy is email marketing — deliverability, list hygiene, engagement, compliance?

Type: Assessment Confidence: 0.86 Sources: 6 Verified: 2026-03-10

Purpose

This assessment evaluates the health of a company's email marketing program across five critical dimensions: deliverability and authentication, list hygiene and growth, engagement and performance, automation and lifecycle, and compliance and privacy. The output is a composite health score (1-5) that pinpoints where the email program is leaking value. [src1]

Constraints

  • Requires ESP analytics access and at least 3 months of send history
  • Open rates unreliable due to Apple MPP inflating opens by 30-40% — use click and conversion rates
  • Deliverability benchmarks vary by ESP, industry, and sending volume
  • Assessment is diagnostic only — does not prescribe campaign strategies
  • Re-run quarterly; email health can degrade rapidly after list purchases or ESP migrations

Common Pitfalls in Assessment

  • Open rate reliance: Apple MPP inflates opens by 30-40%. Use click rates and conversions as primary engagement signals. [src3]
  • Vanity list size: A large list is not a healthy list. Assess list quality by engagement rate, not subscriber count.
  • Compliance complacency: CAN-SPAM alone is insufficient for companies with EU customers. GDPR requires explicit opt-in with audit trail.
  • Authentication set-and-forget: DMARC/SPF/DKIM require ongoing monitoring as sending infrastructure changes.
