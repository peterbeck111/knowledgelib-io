Marketing Technology Spending Benchmarks 2026

What are martech spending benchmarks 2026 — stack costs, category allocation, ROI by company size?

Type: Benchmark Data Vintage: Q1-Q2 2025 Confidence: 0.82 Sources: 6 Verified: 2026-03-11

Summary

Marketing technology accounts for 22.4% of total marketing budgets in 2025, down from 25.4% in 2024, as CMOs shift spend toward paid media (30.6%). The global martech market reached $589 billion. The Gartner CMO Spend Survey found marketing budgets flat at 7.7% of revenue, while martech utilization continues to decline — marketers use only 33% of stack capabilities, down from 58% in 2020. License costs represent only 30-40% of true total cost of ownership. [src1, src4]

Data vintage: Based on Gartner CMO Spend Survey (February-March 2025) and Q4 2025 vendor pricing data.

Key shift: Martech budget share declined to 22.4% as paid media grew to 30.6%. AI investments are the primary growth area, with 49% of marketers reporting improved time efficiency from GenAI.

Constraints

  • Gartner survey covers 402 CMOs at predominantly $1B+ revenue companies. Mid-market and SMB budgets differ significantly.
  • Martech utilization at 33% means significant waste. Raw spending figures overstate effective investment by 2-3x.
  • Global market size ($589B) includes all marketing software. Individual company spend varies from $30K to $8M+.
  • License costs are only 30-40% of true TCO. Integration, staffing, and maintenance multiply actual costs 2-3x.
  • Data from Q1-Q2 2025. Rapid AI proliferation and vendor consolidation may shift 2026 allocations.

Common Misinterpretations

  • Treating license cost as total cost: License fees are 30-40% of TCO. A $100K stack actually costs $250K-$300K with integration and staffing. Always use TCO for budgeting.
  • Assuming more tools = more capability: Average enterprise uses 91 tools but utilizes only 33% of capabilities. Adding tools without improving utilization creates waste.
  • Applying enterprise benchmarks to SMBs: Gartner's 22.4% is for $1B+ companies. SMBs allocate 15-20% with fundamentally different stack compositions.
  • Conflating market size with company spend: The $589B market includes all marketing software globally. Individual spend ranges from $30K to $8M+.
