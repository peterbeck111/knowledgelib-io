Common Misinterpretations

  • 100% utilization as ideal: Above 85% leads to burnout and attrition. Optimal is 70-80%.
  • Comparing utilization across firm types: IT services at 75% is healthy; same for a consulting firm with heavy BD is exceptional.
  • Ignoring utilization denominator: 1,800 hrs (EU) vs 2,080 hrs (US) produces different percentages for same billable hours.
  • Confusing gross vs project margin: Project margin only includes direct costs; gross margin includes all labor. Project margin is typically 5-10pp higher.
